Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2025

1. Introduction

This application allows users to record, transcribe, and summarize meetings. We value your privacy and aim to be transparent about how your data is handled.

2. What Data We Process

We temporarily process the following information:

  • Meeting audio (uploaded from your device)
  • Meeting name (provided by you)
  • Email address (used to send the summary)
  • Transcription and summary (generated during the session)

None of this data is stored permanently. We do not use a database or persistent storage for recordings, transcripts, or summaries.

3. Data Retention

All data is processed in-memory or temporarily saved to disk only for the purpose of transcription and summarization. Temporary files are deleted immediately after processing.

4. Third-Party Services

This app uses OpenAI services to transcribe and summarize your audio, and Google's Gmail API (if authorized) to send the results to your email. Your data is never shared beyond these purposes.

5. Security

We follow best practices to ensure your data is handled securely during processing.

6. Your Consent

By using this application, you consent to the temporary use of your data as described above.
